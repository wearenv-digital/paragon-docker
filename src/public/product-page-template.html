The BSOS-1Z4I-I68 is a high-quality, 720p HD resolution colour video camera with a 4x motorized zoom lens and auto-iris. It includes integrated IR LEDs for operation at 0 lux.

This high-resolution camera supports multiple streams and resolutions in MJPEG or H.264 codec. The zoom lens is remotely adjustable from 2.8 to 12.0 mm, to provide a horizontal field-of-
view of up to 100 degrees.

The camera is housed in a stainless-steel dome rated IP68. It comes ready for mounting and requires only a powered Ethernet network connection.
Features:
• 4x motorized zoom lens
• Day/Night switching for almost any lighting condition
• 264, MJPEG
• PoE
• IP68 Stainless steel enclosure for harsh environments
