Marine Categories
Paragon Security’s marine division – Paragon Marine - has the products and experience to address the challenges of maritime and oil & gas applications with a wide range of rugged products for demanding environments. Designed to withstand the most corrosive atmosphere, our AISI316L PTZ and fixed cameras can be installed on board all types of vessels including oil
tankers, container ships, LNG carriers and naval vessels.

Corrosive seawater can travel in the form of mist, fog and sea spray. Paragon’s CCTV cameras are ideal for installation in ports, coastguard stations, docking, engine rooms, cargo, vessels, boats, and yachts.

Marine Fixed Bullet Stainless Steel CCTV Cameras
Marine Fixed Bullet Marine Finish CCTV Cameras
Marine Fixed Dome Stainless Steel CCTV Cameras
Marine PTZ CCTV Cameras
Exterior Fixed Bullet Stainless Steel CCTV Cameras
